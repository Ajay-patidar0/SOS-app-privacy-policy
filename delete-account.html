Delete Your Account and Data

Effective Date: August 14, 2025

If you would like to delete your SnapSOS account and all associated data, please read the following carefully.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to our support team using the email address below. Include your registered phone number or email so we can verify your identity:

Email: ajaypatidar.it@gmail.com

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your account and login credentials (Firebase Authentication)
  • Saved emergency contacts stored in Firestore
  • Location data associated with SOS events (e.g., coordinates embedded in SOS messages/metadata)
  • App configuration data linked to your user ID (e.g., preferences)

Data Retention Policy

Once we receive a valid deletion request, all associated data will be deleted within 7 days. No personally identifiable information will be retained after this period.

Need Help?

If you face any issues or need help with your request, feel free to reach out to us at ajaypatidar.it@gmail.com.

Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • If you signed in with Google, we will delete your app-side account; you may also manage your Google account data separately in your Google settings.
  • If you re-install or re-register after deletion, it will be treated as a new account.
